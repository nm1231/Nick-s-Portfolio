Artist Statement
My art is about my creative journey throughout my college career. I have curated a portfolio of some of my defining moments and creative breakthroughs throughout my journey. I have created an assortment of each project depending on the class I created it in, and below is a statement that describes what the major lesson I learned from each project was. I also display defining moments I worked on while completing side projects, and I describe what I learned from each art medium I worked with. I combined my graphic design with my digital media art to highlight the similarities and differences between the two mediums. Both mediums are essentially very broad and can be taken in many directions. I hope that my art inspires and helps viewers realize the potential of both of these practices. My art is going to demonstrate the parallels and taste I try to incorporate in them, and also demonstrate the diverging direction I chose to go as an attempt to grow.
